The complainant, Mrs. Kavita Rao, alleges that her brother-in-law, Mr. Vijay Rao, forcibly evicted her from the ancestral home after her husband's death, claiming it solely belonged to his family.

Point No.2:
The Opposite Party (Vijay Rao) is directed –

(i) To reinstate Kavita Rao’s legal right to the property.
(ii) To compensate ₹5,00,000 for emotional and financial hardship.
(iii) To bear litigation expenses.
