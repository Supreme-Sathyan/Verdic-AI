This complaint is filed under Section 12 of the Consumer Protection Act, 2019, praying this Commission to direct the Opposite Party (i) to compensate the complainant for the damages incurred during the test drive; (ii) to repair the damage to the demo car; (iii) to bear the costs of the case.

The complainant, Mr. Ravi Shankar, test-drove a demo car (model XYZ) at ABC Motors, and during the test drive, the car was damaged due to the negligence of the dealership's employee. The complainant alleges that the employee was not careful while handling the car and failed to ensure it was in a safe condition.

Point No.2:

In the result, the complaint is partly allowed, and the Opposite Party (ABC Motors) is directed –

(i) To compensate the complainant with ₹2,50,000 for the damages caused to the demo car.

(ii) To repair the car to its original condition at their expense.

(iii) To bear the costs of litigation incurred by the complainant.
