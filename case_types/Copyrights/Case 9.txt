The complainant, Ms. Meera Kapoor, claims that the streaming platform ABC Originals copied her concept for a web series titled "Dark Maze", without giving her due credit.

Point No.2:
The Opposite Party (ABC Originals) is directed –

(i) To pay ₹75,00,000 in damages for plagiarism.
(ii) To stop broadcasting the web series until legal resolution.
(iii) To provide screenwriting credit to Ms. Kapoor.
