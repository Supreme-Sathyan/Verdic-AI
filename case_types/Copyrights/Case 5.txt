The complainant, Mr. Ankit Verma, states that a leading TV channel Star Networks copied his script for the serial "Broken Bonds" without seeking permission.

Point No.2:
The Opposite Party (Star Networks) is directed –

(i) To pay ₹60,00,000 in damages.
(ii) To halt the airing of the TV serial.
(iii) To provide scriptwriting credit to Mr. Verma.
