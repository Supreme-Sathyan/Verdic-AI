This complaint is filed under Section 12 of the Civil Procedure Code, praying this Hon’ble Court to direct the Opposite Party (i) to recognize the complainant’s rightful share in the ancestral agricultural land; (ii) to prevent illegal encroachment; (iii) to bear the costs of the case.

The complainant, Mr. Rajesh Kumar, claims that after their father’s death, his elder brother, Mr. Anil Kumar, took full control of the ancestral farmland, denying Rajesh’s legal share. Despite multiple requests, Anil refused to divide the land fairly.

Point No.2:
In the result, the complaint is partly allowed, and the Opposite Party (Anil Kumar) is directed –

(i) To legally divide the agricultural land as per inheritance laws.
(ii) To compensate Rajesh Kumar for loss of income due to unlawful possession.
