The complainant, Graphic Designer Sneha Sharma, alleges that the production house DreamWorks India used her poster design for their film "Midnight Echo", without paying for the work.

Point No.2:
The Opposite Party (DreamWorks India) is directed –

(i) To pay ₹10,00,000 in design compensation.
(ii) To issue an official apology for the unauthorized use.
(iii) To bear litigation costs.
