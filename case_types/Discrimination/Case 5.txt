This complaint is filed under Section 12 of the Consumer Protection Act, 2019, praying this Commission to direct the Opposite Party (i) to employ the complainant without discrimination; (ii) to compensate the complainant for lost wages; (iii) to bear the costs of the case.

The complainant, Mr. Ibrahim Khan, was passed over for a promotion at JKL Ltd., despite being the top performer in his department. He believes that the decision was made due to his Muslim faith, as senior management made derogatory comments about his religion.

Point No.2:

In the result, the complaint is partly allowed, and the Opposite Party (JKL Ltd.) is directed –

(i) To compensate the complainant with ₹7,00,000 for the lost promotion and wages.

(ii) To bear the costs of litigation incurred by the complainant.
