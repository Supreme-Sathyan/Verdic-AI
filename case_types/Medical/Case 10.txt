The complainant, Mr. Arjun Rao, alleges that the Opposite Party, Dr. Kunal Sharma, has been running a medical clinic without a valid degree. After a misdiagnosis led to severe complications in the complainant’s health, further investigation revealed that Dr. Sharma never completed medical school.

Point No.2:
The Opposite Party (Dr. Kunal Sharma) is directed –
(i) To shut down the clinic immediately.
(ii) To compensate ₹10 lakhs for medical negligence.
(iii) To face legal action under the Indian Medical Council Act, 1956.
