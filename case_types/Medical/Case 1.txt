The complainant, NGO JanArogya, filed a case against GlobalCare Hospital, which refused to admit a poor patient despite government policies requiring free emergency care. The patient, suffering from a severe lung infection, died outside the hospital gates.

Point No.2:
The Opposite Party (GlobalCare Hospital) is directed –
(i) To pay ₹50 lakhs as compensation to the victim’s family.
(ii) To face legal penalties for violating Article 21 of the Constitution (Right to Life).
(iii) To implement free treatment policies as per the Public Health Act.
