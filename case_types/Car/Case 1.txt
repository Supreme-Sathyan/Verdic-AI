This complaint is filed under Section 12 of the Consumer Protection Act, 2019, praying this Commission to direct the Opposite Party (i) to compensate for the damage caused to the demo car; (ii) to bear the costs of repairs; (iii) to bear the costs of the case.

The complainant, Mr. Sameer Jain, took a demo car for a test drive at DEF Cars, but during the drive, the car was accidentally damaged when the dealership's staff failed to properly secure the vehicle in the parking lot, leading to a collision. The complainant alleges that the dealership is responsible for the damages due to their failure to ensure the car was safely handled.

Point No.2:

In the result, the complaint is partly allowed, and the Opposite Party (DEF Cars) is directed –

(i) To compensate the complainant with ₹3,50,000 for the damages caused to the demo car.

(ii) To bear the costs of all repairs necessary to restore the car.

(iii) To bear the costs of litigation incurred by the complainant.
