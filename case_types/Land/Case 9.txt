The complainant, Ms. Sita Devi, alleges that her younger brother, Mr. Ramesh, forged documents and illegally transferred the title of their late father’s house to his name without consent from other legal heirs.

Point No.2:
The Opposite Party (Ramesh) is directed –

(i) To cancel the fraudulent transfer and restore the original title.
(ii) To pay ₹5,00,000 as compensation for legal expenses and emotional distress.
(iii) To face legal action under property fraud laws.
