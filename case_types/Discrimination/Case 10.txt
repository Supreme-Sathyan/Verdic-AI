This complaint is filed under Section 12 of the Consumer Protection Act, 2019, praying this Commission to direct the Opposite Party (i) to reinstate the complainant to her previous position; (ii) to compensate the complainant for lost wages; (iii) to bear the costs of the case and pass such other order or orders as this Hon’ble Commission deems fit and proper.

The complainant, Ms. Rina Verma, was employed as a senior manager at XYZ Ltd. She alleges that she was demoted and passed over for a promotion due to her gender. The position was instead given to a less qualified male employee.

Point No.2:

In the result, the complaint is partly allowed, and the Opposite Party (XYZ Ltd.) is directed –

(i) To reinstate the complainant to her previous position.

(ii) To compensate the complainant with ₹5,00,000 for lost wages.

(iii) To bear the costs of litigation incurred by the complainant.
