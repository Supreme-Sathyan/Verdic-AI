This complaint is filed by the complainant under Section 12 of the Consumer Protection Act, 2019, praying this Commission to direct the Opposite Party (i) to admit the complainant to the university; (ii) to compensate the complainant for the harm caused; (iii) to bear the costs of the case.

The complainant, Ms. Linda Hernandez, an immigrant from Mexico, was rejected by PQR University despite her excellent academic performance. She believes her rejection was based on her national origin, as she was told she "wasn't a good fit."

Point No.2:

In the result, the complaint is partly allowed, and the Opposite Party (PQR University) is directed –

(i) To admit the complainant to the university.

(ii) To compensate the complainant with ₹4,00,000 for the harm caused.

(iii) To bear the costs of litigation incurred by the complainant.
