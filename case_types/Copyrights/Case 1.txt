The complainant, The Estate of Late Actor Rajesh Kapoor, states that a film studio produced a biopic "Kapoor: The Legend", without obtaining consent from the family.

Point No.2:
The Opposite Party (Film Studio) is directed –

(i) To halt the release of the biopic.
(ii) To pay ₹1 crore in damages.
(iii) To obtain legal permission before proceeding further.
