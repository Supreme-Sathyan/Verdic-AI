The complainant, Mr. Sandeep Joshi, was charged ₹5 lakhs for a simple emergency consultation at ABC Private Hospital. The hospital refused to admit him until the full payment was made upfront.

Point No.2:
The Opposite Party (ABC Private Hospital) is directed –
(i) To refund the excess charges with 12% interest.
(ii) To pay ₹10 lakhs as penalty for unethical pricing.
(iii) To comply with government-mandated pricing under the Clinical Establishments Act, 2010.
