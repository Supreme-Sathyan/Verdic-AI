The complainant, Mr. Rajat Gupta, went for a kidney stone operation at City Care Hospital. Later, he found that his left kidney was missing. An internal hospital probe revealed that the organ had been illegally sold to a third party.

Point No.2:
The Opposite Party (City Care Hospital) is directed –
(i) To compensate ₹1 crore to the victim.
(ii) To face criminal prosecution for organ trafficking under the Transplantation of Human Organs Act, 1994.
