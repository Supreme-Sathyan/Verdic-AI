The complainant, Mr. Ramesh Gupta, states that after their parents’ death, his elder sister, Ms. Meera Gupta, claimed full ownership of their parental home, refusing to recognize Ramesh’s legal share.

Point No.2:

(i) To legally partition the house between all siblings.
(ii) To compensate Ramesh Gupta with ₹3,00,000 for wrongful exclusion.
(iii) To bear litigation expenses.
