The complainant, Mrs. Sunita Deshmukh, called an ambulance for her father, who suffered a cardiac arrest. The ambulance from LifeLine Services arrived 90 minutes late, by which time the patient had passed away.

Point No.2:
The Opposite Party (LifeLine Services) is directed –
(i) To compensate ₹20 lakhs for negligence.
(ii) To ensure response times do not exceed 15 minutes in urban areas.
(iii) To face investigation under the Emergency Medical Services Act.
