This complaint is filed under Section 12 of the Consumer Protection Act, 2019, praying this Commission to direct the Opposite Party (i) to rent the apartment to the complainant without discrimination; (ii) to compensate the complainant for the inconvenience caused; (iii) to bear the costs of the case.

The complainant, Mr. Dev Kumar, was denied the opportunity to rent an apartment at XYZ Housing Complex due to his lower socioeconomic status. He had the financial means to afford the apartment but was told that the landlord only accepted "affluent tenants."

Point No.2:

In the result, the complaint is partly allowed, and the Opposite Party (XYZ Housing Complex) is directed –

(i) To offer the complainant a rental agreement for the apartment.

(ii) To compensate the complainant with ₹3,00,000 for the inconvenience.

(iii) To bear the costs of litigation incurred by the complainant.
