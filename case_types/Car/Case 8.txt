This complaint is filed under Section 12 of the Consumer Protection Act, 2019, praying this Commission to direct the Opposite Party (i) to compensate the complainant for the damages caused due to poor road construction; (ii) to repair the vehicle at their cost; (iii) to bear the costs of the case.

The complainant, Mr. Rakesh Sharma, was driving on a newly constructed road when his car suffered damage from an uncovered drainage pit. The complainant claims that the construction company did not put up warning signs or barriers around the dangerous area, leading to the damage.

Point No.2:

In the result, the complaint is partly allowed, and the Opposite Party (XYZ Construction Pvt. Ltd.) is directed –

(i) To compensate the complainant with ₹75,000 for the damage to the car.

(ii) To cover the costs of repairing the vehicle.

(iii) To bear the costs of litigation incurred by the complainant.
