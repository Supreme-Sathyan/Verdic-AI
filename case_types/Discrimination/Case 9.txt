This complaint is filed by the complainant under Section 12 of the Consumer Protection Act, 2019, praying this Commission to direct the Opposite Party (i) to rent the apartment to the complainant without discrimination; (ii) to compensate the complainant for the inconvenience caused; (iii) to bear the costs of the case.

The complainant, Mr. Ahmed Khan, was refused a rental agreement by ABC Properties because of his racial background. Despite having the financial means to afford the rent and providing all necessary documents, he was told the apartment was "no longer available."

Point No.2:

In the result, the complaint is partly allowed, and the Opposite Party (ABC Properties) is directed –

(i) To offer the complainant a rental agreement for the available apartment.

(ii) To compensate the complainant with ₹2,50,000 for the inconvenience.

(iii) To bear the costs of litigation incurred by the complainant.
