The complainant, Digital Entertainment Ltd., claims that a streaming service, "PrimeFlix", uploaded their hit TV show "Legends of the West", without securing licensing rights.

Point No.2:
The Opposite Party (PrimeFlix) is directed –

(i) To remove the TV show immediately.
(ii) To pay ₹1.5 crores in damages for illegal streaming.
(iii) To bear litigation costs.
