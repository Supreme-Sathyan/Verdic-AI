The complainant, Comic Artist Ravi Sharma, alleges that the movie "Firestorm" copied the main character from his comic "Inferno Man", violating copyright laws.

Point No.2:
The Opposite Party (Film Production Company) is directed –

(i) To pay ₹40,00,000 for character infringement.
(ii) To acknowledge Ravi Sharma’s original creation.
(iii) To cease further merchandising of the copied character.
