The complainant, NGO Health Rights Association, discovered that a hospital was running a fake COVID-19 vaccination camp, administering saline water instead of real vaccines. Over 1,000 people were affected.

Point No.2:
The Opposite Party (XYZ Hospital) is directed –
(i) To compensate ₹2 crores to the victims.
(ii) To face criminal charges for fraud and endangering public health.
(iii) To be blacklisted under the National Medical Commission Act, 2019.
