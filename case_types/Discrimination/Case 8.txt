This complaint is filed under Section 12 of the Consumer Protection Act, 2019, praying this Commission to direct the Opposite Party (i) to provide reasonable accommodation to the complainant in the workplace; (ii) to compensate the complainant for wrongful termination; (iii) to bear the costs of the case.

The complainant, Mr. Sanjay Gupta, was fired from his job at DEF Corp after he disclosed his disability. He had been performing well but was denied necessary accommodations to perform his duties effectively, leading to his eventual dismissal.

Point No.2:

In the result, the complaint is partly allowed, and the Opposite Party (DEF Corp) is directed –

(i) To reinstate the complainant to his previous position with reasonable accommodations.

(ii) To compensate the complainant with ₹4,00,000 for wrongful termination.

(iii) To bear the costs of litigation incurred by the complainant.
