This complaint is filed under Section 12 of the Consumer Protection Act, 2019, praying this Commission to direct the Opposite Party (i) to hire the complainant for the vacant position; (ii) to compensate the complainant for lost income; (iii) to bear the costs of the case.

The complainant, Mr. Rajesh Mehra, a 58-year-old applicant, was refused a job opportunity at XYZ Technologies despite having more experience than the younger candidates hired for the role. The company stated they were looking for "young talent."

Point No.2:

In the result, the complaint is partly allowed, and the Opposite Party (XYZ Technologies) is directed –

(i) To offer the complainant a position suitable for his qualifications.

(ii) To compensate the complainant with ₹6,00,000 for lost income.

(iii) To bear the costs of litigation incurred by the complainant.
