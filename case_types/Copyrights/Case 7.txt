The complainant, Music Composer Arjun Nair, claims that a Bollywood film "City Hustle" used his original composition without his consent.

Point No.2:
The Opposite Party (Film Producer) is directed –

(i) To pay ₹30,00,000 for unauthorized usage.
(ii) To officially credit Arjun Nair as the composer.
(iii) To cease further use of the background score.
