This complaint is filed under Section 12 of the Consumer Protection Act, 2019, praying this Commission to direct the Opposite Party (i) to compensate the complainant for the damage to the vehicle during service; (ii) to repair the damage at their own cost; (iii) to bear the costs of the case.

The complainant, Mr. Akash Patel, took his car for a routine service at XYZ Auto Workshop. Upon receiving the car, Mr. Patel noticed significant scratches on the bodywork and a dent in the rear bumper. The service center failed to acknowledge the damage or offer compensation.

Point No.2:

In the result, the complaint is partly allowed, and the Opposite Party (XYZ Auto Workshop) is directed –

(i) To compensate the complainant with ₹50,000 for the damages to the car.

(ii) To repair the dent and scratches at their own expense.

(iii) To bear the costs of litigation incurred by the complainant.
