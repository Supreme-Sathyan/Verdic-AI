This complaint is filed under Section 12 of the Consumer Protection Act, 2019, praying this Commission to direct the Opposite Party (i) to reinstate the complainant to her position; (ii) to compensate the complainant for lost wages and emotional distress; (iii) to bear the costs of the case.

The complainant, Ms. Priya Rao, was fired from her position as a marketing executive at MNO Corp. after informing her employer of her pregnancy. Despite having excellent performance reviews, she was told her pregnancy "would interfere with her work."

Point No.2:

In the result, the complaint is partly allowed, and the Opposite Party (MNO Corp.) is directed –

(i) To reinstate the complainant to her position.

(ii) To compensate the complainant with ₹6,00,000 for lost wages and emotional distress.

(iii) To bear the costs of litigation incurred by the complainant.
